About BOLTT

BOLTT exists to put people back in control of their own infrastructure. The cloud made computing easy, but it also handed the keys to your data over to companies and countries you will never meet. We think that trade was a mistake, and we are building the tools to undo it.

Our Mission

Make running your own sovereign cloud as simple as signing up for someone else's. You own the hardware, you own the data, and you decide who touches it. No foreign data centers, no opaque access policies, no quiet hand-offs to a third party.

We believe sovereignty should not require a team of specialists. It should require a power outlet and a few minutes of your time.

What We Believe

  • Your data belongs to you, not to whoever rents you a server.
  • Security and compliance should be the default, not an upsell.
  • Good infrastructure should be understandable by the people who depend on it.
  • The hard parts of system administration can be handled by AI that actually knows your system and is run locally on hardware that you own.

Where We Are Headed

Today BOLTT is a curated, pre-configured stack of proven open-source infrastructure, woven together and made approachable by Ebi, our AI administrator. Tomorrow it becomes BOLTT OS: a single, unified operating system for sovereign cloud, where the stack disappears and you are left with one cohesive product that simply works.

We are not there yet, but every release moves us closer. If you believe infrastructure should answer to you and no one else, you are already one of us.
