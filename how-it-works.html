How BOLTT Works

From rack to running in three steps.

1. Deploy

Rack the hardware, plug it in. We install BOLTT on your hardware. No multi-day setup, no specialist team required.

2. Configure

Ebi auto-discovers your systems and sets baseline security and compliance policies. Tweak anything in plain English instead of clicking through a hundred admin panels.

3. Operate

Ask Ebi anything. Generate reports, audit access, patch systems, monitor security — all by talking to her like you would a senior admin.

How BOLTT Actually Works

BOLTT is a curated stack of proven open-source infrastructure. virtualization, identity, networking, storage, observability — integrated and pre-configured so you don't have to glue them together yourself. It runs on hardware you own, in a location you control.

Ebi is the AI woven through every layer. She has access to the same telemetry, logs, and system state that a senior systems administrator would, and she uses it to answer questions, generate reports, and execute changes in plain English. She's the most important part of BOLTT. The layer that makes everything else approachable.

The endgame is BOLTT OS: a single unified operating system that replaces the curated stack with one cohesive product. We're not there yet. What we have today is the foundation. and Ebi makes it work like an OS already.
